Na Pegada do Saci

Experiments with P5.js

Here are simple sketches made most as learning P5. Although they are simple I thought it would be worth opening them to the world. Hopefully the list of sketches will grow endlessly.


Some of the sketches are interactive and some are not. There's a tooltip in the links at the navbar indicating which is interactive and how to interact with it.


Everyone is invited to make sketches and see them here.
The way of doing that is by making a pull request on the repository on GitHub.
Have fun.
